About us

DOWNTOWN is a lifestyle magazine highlighting the unique and exciting culture of North Jersey. DOWNTOWN provides smart, hip and stylish coverage of everything that our readers care about including family, health, dining, cooking, shopping, style, history and sports.

Our pages are entertaining, sophisticated and informative. One of our goals is to enhance the lives of our readers by highlighting the unique and interesting people—your friends and neighbors—who help make your neighborhood such a great place to live, work and gather.

Life in North Jersey is fast-paced and exciting. But it’s also a place where family comes first and people enjoy taking the time to smell the roses. That’s why DOWNTOWN magazine will always be a place where people can just be themselves, kick back and have a great time.

Why advertise with us?

For 15 years, Downtown has been celebrating life experiences for people of all ages, while delivering relatable content that’s entertaining and useful. Turning a page in Downtown means encountering a friend or a neighbor. That includes our advertisers. Some of the businesses and business owners you’ll read about in Downtown are also your friends and neighbors. That familiarity and instant recognition is one of the keys to successful advertising.

Businesses that are at the top of consumers’ minds have a high market share. Consistent, creative advertising will get your business top-of-mind awareness. You may not achieve the brand awareness of Kleenex or Hershey, but you can be the first business local consumers think of when buying products or services in your category. That’s what Downtown magazine can do for you with consistent, creative advertising.
